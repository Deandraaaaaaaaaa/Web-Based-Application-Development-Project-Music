The Emptiness Machine

Linkin Park

Producer: Mike Shinoda

“The Emptiness Machine” is the first song by Linkin Park that was made since the passing of Chester Bennington in 2017, as well as their first song to feature Emily Armstrong as the new lead vocalist and Colin Brittain as the new drummer in the band.
